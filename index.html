About the Work

Immanuel works across acrylic, charcoal, and digital illustration, building an Afrofuturist portraiture practice rooted in West African visual heritage.

The work moves between painted surface and digital layer, using each medium's own texture — the grain of charcoal, the flatness of digital colour, the weight of acrylic — as part of what the image says.
